Seek Help When You Need It

If you're stuck and need legal or emergency help, there are many laws and helplines in place to protect women from any form of violence or abuse. Know your rights and take action.

Government Helplines for Women

  • Women Helpline (All India) - 1091: Emergency response for women facing harassment or violence.
  • Women Helpline (Domestic Abuse) - 181: Provides assistance to women facing domestic violence.
  • Child Helpline - 1098: Assistance for children in distress, including girls in abusive situations.
  • Police - 100: Immediate police response for emergencies.
  • National Commission for Women (NCW): Handles women's rights violations and complaints.

Laws Protecting Women in India

  • The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act (2013): Protects women from workplace harassment.
  • Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act (2005): Safeguards women against domestic violence.
  • The Dowry Prohibition Act (1961): Prohibits the giving or taking of dowry.
  • The Indecent Representation of Women (Prohibition) Act (1986): Prevents the indecent portrayal of women.
  • Section 498A IPC: Protects married women from cruelty by husbands or their families.
