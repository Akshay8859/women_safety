SURVEILLANCE CAMERAS

Indian Government has about 1.5 million surveillance cameras installed across 15 cities.

FUN FACT

Telangana is the most surveilled state in India with 36 CCTV cameras per 1,000 people, while cities Delhi and Chennai have more cameras per square mile than cities in China.[.

Future of surveillance

The overall Indian video surveillance market, which includes public and private sector spending, is expected to reach over $4 billion by 2029.

"CCTNS"

Crime and Criminal Tracking Network & Systems project, which was launched in 2009. It aims to connect over 15,000 police stations across the country, facilitating real-time access to crime and criminal data.
